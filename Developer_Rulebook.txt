## **SmolLyfe – Development Goal and Mission Charter**

### **Purpose**

*SmolLyfe* is a systemic life simulator built to reproduce the complexity of human growth, emotion, and consequence through interacting mechanics — not scripted storytelling.
The aim is to create a cohesive, data-driven world where every decision, relationship, and milestone dynamically alters the simulation’s trajectory.

---

### **Mission**

To develop an evolving simulation where:

* Systems communicate with each other in real time — no mechanic stands alone.
* Lives feel organic because outcomes arise from internal logic, not fixed storylines.
* The framework remains expandable, allowing new life systems, professions, and relationships to integrate seamlessly over time.

---

### **Core Design Pillars**

1. **Interconnection** — Every domain (career, family, fame, relationships, health) feeds into the others through shared values such as stress, time, morality, and reputation.
2. **Authenticity** — Emotional realism matters more than numerical optimization. Growth, loss, and chance all play equal narrative roles.
3. **Scalability** — The simulation must remain modular, supporting future systems like economy, global events, or generational legacy without rewriting foundations.
4. **Replayability through Emergence** — Events should arise from player context and history rather than repetition or branching scripts.
5. **Balance over Bias** — All advantages have proportional risks; fame, wealth, or power introduce trade-offs that preserve realism.

* Workload influences health and relationships.
* Each occupation can intersect with others through scandals, fame events, or investigations.

* Retirement, burnout, or moral collapse are valid narrative endpoints.

**Development Guidelines**

* Every feature must interact with at least one existing mechanic.
* Events and milestones should always contain cause, effect, and feedback.
* Randomization is weighted by context — personality, fame, health, and stress.
* Balance outcomes; success and loss must coexist in believable proportion.
* Expand depth first, then breadth — reinforce what already exists before adding new systems.

**Vision for Future Iterations**

* Fame, wealth, and morality reshape how the world responds.

* Future modules — mental health, global events, or societal systems — integrate seamlessly into the same life loop.
